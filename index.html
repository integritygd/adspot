How it works

Two sides, one simple platform

Whether you've got space to rent or a business to grow, AdSpot makes it a few taps — and our crews do the heavy lifting.

For space owners

Earn money from space you already own

  • 1List your wall, fence, lot, window, or screen — a few photos and an address.
  • 2Set your price (or let us suggest one based on foot/road traffic).
  • 3Approve advertisers you like. We install everything.
  • 4Get paid automatically, every month.
